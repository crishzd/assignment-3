About Us – Paws & Care Pet Minders

At Paws & Care Dog Minders, our passion for pets runs deep. As a team of dedicated animal lovers, we believe in offering more than just pet care—we create a home away from home for your furry friends. Our goal is to provide peace of mind to pet owners by ensuring their dogs receive the highest level of care, attention, and love while they’re away.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every pet with the same love and care we would our own. Your dog will always feel safe and cherished in our hands.
  • Trust: We know that trust is essential when it comes to pet care. Our team works hard to build lasting relationships with both pets and their owners.
  • Community: We’re more than just a pet care service—we’re a community of pet lovers who share a common bond. We encourage pet owners to stay connected with us, whether through social media or in-person visits.

When you choose Paws & Care Dog Minders, you're not just choosing a place for your pet to stay. You're choosing a team that genuinely cares about your dog’s well-being and happiness. With our customized care plans, safe environment, and loving staff, we’re here to ensure your dog feels right at home, every time.

Thank you for trusting us with your furry family members. We look forward to being part of your dog’s journey!
